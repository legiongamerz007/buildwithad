Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI services

What types of AI solutions do you offer?

We offer comprehensive AI solutions including intelligent agents, AI-powered applications, conversational chatbots, AI-driven games, and cross-platform game development. Our services are tailored to meet specific business needs and objectives.

How long does it take to develop an AI solution?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple chatbots can be ready in 2-4 weeks, while complex AI applications may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation phase.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages. This includes regular updates, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and feature enhancements to ensure your AI solution continues to deliver optimal results.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on the complexity, features, and timeline. We offer flexible payment plans and provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements. Contact us for a personalized estimate.

Can you work with existing systems and data?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating AI solutions with existing systems and databases. Our team ensures seamless integration while maintaining data security and system performance.

What industries do you serve?

We serve clients across various industries including healthcare, finance, e-commerce, education, gaming, and manufacturing. Our AI solutions are adaptable to any industry's specific needs and challenges.
