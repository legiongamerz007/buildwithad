Our Story

BuildWithAD was founded in 2019 by Dr. Sarah Chen, a visionary AI researcher who recognized the immense potential of artificial intelligence to transform businesses across industries.

What started as a small team of passionate AI enthusiasts has grown into a leading technology company, serving clients worldwide with innovative AI solutions. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Today, we're proud to have delivered over 150 successful projects, helping businesses leverage the power of AI to achieve their goals and stay ahead of the competition.
