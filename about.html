About

Hi, I'm Adeel Ahmad

Associate Engineering Manager by day, solo engineering studio by night. I build Shopify stores for global brands and ship my own products — from health tech to AI game tools.

I'm a full-stack engineer based in Pakistan, working remotely with clients in the US, UK, Germany, Netherlands, and France. Over the past several years I've delivered 24+ Shopify and e-commerce projects — custom themes, full store builds, and performance work for jewelry, wellness, fashion, and health brands.

By day I lead engineering at ARVO, where I focus on agentic software delivery and helping teams ship faster with AI-assisted development. BuildWithAD is my client-facing studio — one person, direct communication, production-quality code.

Beyond client work, I'm building a portfolio of products: GlycoRange (a live diabetes tracker at glycorange.com), GDDforge and Playforge (AI game design tools), Life Hub (a personal business OS), and ventures in derma/beauty and education CMS with partners.

I believe in honest scoping, fixed milestones, and shipping — not endless discovery phases. If I take your project, you'll work directly with me from first call to launch.
